What is this?

This is the page for interacting with Internet Archive through our archiving service OSF Pigeon!

Workflow Info

Workflow for IA: When setting up our environment for IA we must follow a few simple steps.

  1. Make Provider collections/subcollections

    For our system we sync one registration to one IA item, every item exists in a collection that represents it's provider so a OSF registration will be in a collection titled `OSF Registries` with an url of https://archive.org/details/osf-registration-providers-osf-{{ia_id_version}} . All these provider based collections are themselves subcollections of our "root collection" ({{ ia_collection }}) The root collection exists to separate our staging, local and production environments. In order to create/update the provider collection click Create/Update Provider SubCollections The root collections must be created by an archive.org admin.

  2. Archive backlogged registrations

    Once we've created our collections it's time to start filling them with registrations. Due to the large number of registrations we are planning to gradually pull registrations from a backlog of unarchived registration. There's a task that runs every night and archives a batch of registrations. However if a registration is skipped or broken, the archive job can be restarted by clicking the Trigger IA Archive button with it's guid. backlogged registrations here

  3. Monitor syncing

    Once a registration is archived the editable metadata should be synced automatically, but with so many registrations there's still the possibility that a registration will become desynchronized due to a connection outage or glitch, so there's tools to sync metadata or check it for accuracy
